Our Story

A passion for excellence, a commitment to sustainability

A Vision Realized

Founded in 2024 by acclaimed Chef Marcus Chen, Veridian was born from a desire to create a dining experience that transcends the ordinary. With a background in Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe and Asia, Chef Chen envisioned a space where culinary innovation meets genuine hospitality.

Our name, Veridian, reflects the lush coastal landscapes that inspire our cuisine. We believe in honoring the natural beauty of ingredients, allowing their purest flavors to shine through thoughtful preparation and artistic presentation.

Each dish tells a story of collaboration with local farmers, fishermen, and artisans who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Meet the Chef

Marcus Chen

With over 15 years of experience in world-renowned kitchens, Chef Marcus brings a unique perspective that merges classical French techniques with Asian influences. His culinary philosophy is simple: respect the ingredient, honor the tradition, embrace creativity.

Before opening Veridian, Marcus served as Executive Chef at Michelin-starred restaurants in Paris, Tokyo, and New York. His accolades include James Beard "Rising Star" and recognition as one of Food & Wine's "Best New Chefs."

"Cooking is my language of love."
